01) CAN WE RESERVE TAXI'S EVEN DURING THE COVID OUTBREAK?

YES - We are still transporting customers across the country. You will be required to provide a recent test (72 hour max) result that saying negative for Covid-19.
02) HOW FAR IN ADVANCE CAN I BOOK A TAXI?

Minimum time is 30 minutes in a city area. For rural areas we recommend at least 2 hours. Time can vary because it's completely depend on the avaliable drivers and their locatins. The sooner you can is the better.
03) IF I HAVE A RETURN TRIP, CAN I BOOK IN THE SAME PAGE?

NO, You must book it as a separate trip from our website.
04) WHAT CAN I DO IF MY DRIVER ASKED FOR EXTRA MONEY THAN THE TRIP FARE?

All our fares include tips and gratitude bonuses. If you like you can tip him. You can IGNORE all other requests as you wish.
05) DO I NEED TO SHARE MY RIDE WITH OTHERS WHO ARE NOT IN MY GROUP?

NO, No matter the vehicle type, it's exclusively for your journey only.
